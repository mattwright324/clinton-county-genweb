    Lookups are provided on a volunteer basis by patrons who will attempt to answer your brief genealogical or historical question regarding the families or communities of White County, Indiana utilizing the resources they have available to them.  

    Lookups may be requested by contacting the following volunteers:

       Trudy Johnson  

    Dana Palmer Limited to: Brookston IOOF Cemetery and any marriage reference (1834-1928)

    • PLEASE NOTE! While individual look-ups can be requested it is in the best interest for all visitors and interested researchers to post all lookup requests to the White County Discussion Board so that everyone can benefit from the questions and answers, These boards are also monitored by the lookup volunteers along with many people around the country with a wealth of knowledge and family relation to White County - Thanks!
