Clinton County Searchable Cemetery Index

Search the services of "Find-a-grave", Clinton County which is a publicly supported volunteer project to provide a database of cemetery records with features such as the ability to post photographs, biographies, and other information about your loved ones.  Data is constantly being added to this huge project so please check back often and please help out where you can!  ENTER Clinton County Cemetery Records
